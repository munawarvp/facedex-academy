Who We Are

FacadeX Group was born from a vision to revolutionize the façade industry. Founded by three experienced professionals who have been working in the GCC facade sector for over 20 years.
Our journey began when we started a Saudi-based subsidiary company in our homeland Kerala and started working together.

Early on, we faced a major challenge: there was a shortage of skilled façade professionals in the market, making it difficult to execute projects efficiently. At the same time, we saw countless graduates struggling to find jobs due to a lack of practical skills and real-world experience. This gap sparked an idea — to connect aspiring professionals with industry opportunities and elevate the standard of facade engineering. That’s how FacadeX Academy was born.

The Academy focuses on hands-on, practical training, integrating live projects from the GCC and India, ensuring students gain real-world experience and are ready to meet industry demands.
As we grew, many companies in the GCC approached us with pending projects, citing the lack of qualified professionals. We stepped in, successfully completing these projects and building trust with clients — and that’s how FacadeX Consultancy came into existence.

Today, we have trusted partnerships with 10+ companies across the GCC, delivering reliable facade engineering solutions.
To complete the ecosystem, we launched FacadeX Recruitment, connecting skilled professionals with leading facade companies across the GCC and India. This initiative not only helped companies overcome workforce shortages but also created meaningful opportunities for our students and other professionals.

Today, FacadeX Group stands as a trusted hub for learning, consultancy, and recruitment in the facade industry, driven by a mission to empower professionals, support companies, and transform the engineering landscape.

Our Mission

At FacadeX Group, our mission is to bridge the gap between education and industry by creating a complete ecosystem for facade professionals. We aim to empower engineers, designers, and technicians with the right skills, practical exposure, and global opportunities needed to excel in the facade sector. Through our Academy, we deliver hands-on, industry-relevant training; through our Consultancy, we provide innovative, reliable engineering solutions; and through our Recruitment division, we connect skilled professionals with leading companies across the GCC and India. Our ultimate goal is to build a stronger, smarter, and more connected façade industry — one that shapes the skylines of tomorrow.
