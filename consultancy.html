FacadeX Consultancy

FacadeX Engineering Consultancy was established to deliver expert facade engineering solutions to companies across the GCC and Europe. With decades of industry experience, we provide technical excellence, precision, and cost efficiency through services such as design support, shop drawings, and structural analysis.

Our skilled team combines global standards with local insight, ensuring every project meets the highest quality benchmarks. Operating from India, we offer world-class consultancy at competitive costs, helping facade contractors, developers, and builders achieve outstanding results.

At FacadeX, we believe in collaboration, innovation, and reliability — shaping smarter, more efficient facades worldwide.
