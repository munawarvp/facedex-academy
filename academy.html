Training Engineers, Transforming Skylines

Facadex Academy

FacadeX Academy is a specialized training hub dedicated to developing skilled professionals for the façade industry. We bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world application through hands-on training, live project integration, and industry-driven curriculum. Our programs are designed by façade experts with years of GCC experience, ensuring students gain both technical and practical knowledge. From design and detailing to project management, we prepare learners to meet global industry standards. At FacadeX Academy, education goes beyond theory — it’s about building careers that shape skylines.

Our Courses

Diploma in Facade Engineering

6 Months Course + 6 Months Internship Offline

Gain comprehensive knowledge of facade systems, materials, and technical documentation. This diploma is designed for those aspiring to build a strong foundation in facade design and execution.

BIM in Facade

45 Hours Online/Offline

Master facade engineering using Autodesk Revit and BIM (Building Information Modeling) tools. Learn how to implement BIM in facade projects for design coordination, collaboration, and efficiency.

Structural Calculations in Facade

30 Hours Online

Learn the principles of structural analysis in facade systems. This course bridges theory and practical software applications to help you analyze and optimize facade structures safely and efficiently.

Logikal-Orgadata training

40 Hours Online

A complete training program on Orgadata’s LogiKal software, the industry standard for facade and aluminum system design. Learn how to manage projects efficiently from concept to fabrication.

Diploma in Estimation Engineering

60 Hours Online/Offline

Become an expert in facade estimation and cost engineering. Learn how to prepare precise cost analyses, material take-offs, and BOQs to ensure project profitability and efficiency.
